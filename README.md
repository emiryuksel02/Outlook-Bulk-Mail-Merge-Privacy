# Outlook-Bulk-Mail-Merge-Privacy
Send personalized bulk emails from Excel/CSV using your Outlook account – right from your browser.

Privacy Policy – Outlook Bulk Mail Merge

Last updated: 16.03.2026

1. Overview

Outlook Bulk Mail Merge (“the extension”) is developed by CyberLife82.
This extension helps you send personalized bulk emails from an Excel/CSV file using your own Outlook / Microsoft 365 account.

2. What data is processed

When you use the extension, the following data may be processed locally in your browser:

Contact data from your Excel/CSV file (e.g. email addresses, names, company names, URLs, custom fields you define)
Email templates you write (subject and body)
Configuration such as from name, throttle/delay settings, and which column is used as the email address
3. Where data is stored

Contact data and settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome extension storage.
This data is not sent to any server controlled by the developer.
You can clear this data at any time by removing the extension or clearing extension storage.
4. How emails are sent

Emails are sent using your own Outlook / Microsoft 365 account via the official Microsoft Graph API.
The extension uses Microsoft’s OAuth sign‑in pages (login.microsoftonline.com) to obtain an access token with the Mail.Send permission.
Email content and recipient addresses are transmitted directly from your browser to Microsoft (graph.microsoft.com) for the sole purpose of sending your messages.
5. No sale or sharing of data

The developer does not collect, log, sell, or share your contact data or email content.
No analytics or tracking code is added by the extension.
6. Third‑party services

Microsoft may process your data according to its own terms and privacy policy when you use your Outlook / Microsoft 365 account and Microsoft Graph.
Please refer to Microsoft’s own privacy statements for details.
7. Children’s privacy

The extension is intended for use by adults and is not directed to children.
